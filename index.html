🏥 TRACER 2025

A mini app for convenient preparation for periodic accreditation at the Wolfson Medical Center.

I was always a little scared by the huge number of printed sheets of unstructured text that you need to study and remember in a short time. So it was decided to help myself and colleagues. 🙂

✨ Features: light & dark themes, separate question sets for doctors 👨‍⚕️, nurses 👩‍⚕️, and support staff 🧑‍🔬, four languages.

🌐 Translations via Google Translate — may have shortcomings.

🛠 Amateur work — don't judge too harshly.

Good luck! 🍀
